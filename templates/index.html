Reading Is Powerful
Books don’t just fill shelves — they fill your mind, your imagination, and your life. Open one, and you open up a new world.
Why Read?
  • Improves vocabulary (so you sound smarter on dates).
  • Reduces stress (cheaper than therapy).
  • Makes you empathetic (you might even cry for fictional people).
  • Boosts memory and focus (no more “wait what was I doing?”).
  • You look cool reading in cafes (yes, even if it's comics).

“A reader lives a thousand lives before he dies.”

…The man who never reads lives only one. — George R.R. Martin
